Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
KickPick.eu is operated as an independent football predictions platform. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how we handle data.

1. Information we collect

KickPick does not require registration or account creation. We collect minimal data:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type — collected anonymously via Vercel Analytics
  • Cookies: We use cookies strictly necessary for site functionality and, where consented, for advertising (Google AdSense)
  • No personal data: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information

2. How we use data

Anonymous usage data is used solely to understand how visitors use the site and improve the service. We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties except as described below.

3. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google AdSense — displays advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve personalised ads based on your browsing history. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel Analytics — anonymous site analytics. No personal data collected.
  • Google Fonts — fonts served by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary: Required for the site to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the site.
  • Advertising: Used by Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to data portability

As we collect no personally identifiable information, these rights are inherently satisfied. For any queries, contact us at: privacy@kickpick.eu

6. Data retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 12 months. We do not store any personal data.

7. Children

KickPick is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. This site contains betting-related content and is intended for adults only.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of any changes.

9. Contact
